About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Espresso has been dedicated to serving the finest coffee and pastries in a cozy and welcoming atmosphere.
Our passion for quality ingredients and exceptional customer service has made us a beloved spot for coffee enthusiasts and casual visitors alike.
Two decades later, we’ve expanded to over 20 cafes throughout Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, and Westchester, with our very own roastery in Long Island City.

Why Us?

Espresso offers a unique coffee house environment unlike any other in town.
We are a place where you can sit down and enjoy that tailor-made cup of coffee.
We offer a delicious variety of coffee roasted in house made by our professionally trained baristas.
We have everything from classic coffee to our house made specialty beverages.
All of our sauces & syrups are made in-house with all natural ingredients ensuring you the highest quality in taste & health.
You can complete your coffee with one of our delicious sweet treats made by our very own baker.
